Boss #1 · Wrenwood Hotel
CHUNK
Heavily mutated former security guard. First major boss encounter.
Leon Grace (alternate method) 2 Phases
Moderate Difficulty Recommended: Shotgun
Weak Points
🎯
Phase 1: Exposed chest cavity — shoot during his charge wind-up animation
🎯
Phase 2: Glowing mutation on his back — must circle around or use flash grenades to force him to turn
Drops & Rewards
Hatchet: Mortal Edge (Leon) — unequip current hatchet to equip
Chunk's Keycard — required to exit the Hotel basement
3× Shotgun Shells (scattered around arena)
Strategy

Phase 1 Stay mobile. Chunk telegraphs his charge 1.5 seconds before executing — the window to shoot the chest is during the "pull back" animation. Two shotgun blasts or four rifle shots end Phase 1.

Phase 2 He gains a back mutation. Use Flash Grenades or shoot his legs to stagger him and force a 180 — then dump shotgun ammo into the glowing mass. If playing Grace, inject 3 Hemolytic Injectors when the back mutation is exposed to finish the fight instantly.

Grace players: The 3 Hemolytic Injector method skips the Phase 2 damage check entirely. Craft them before the fight by killing the 3 zombies in the hallway preceding the boss room.
Boss #2 · Raccoon City — RPD Chase + Final Encounter
MR. X
Tyrant-series B.O.W. Pursues Leon throughout the RPD. Two distinct fight phases.
Leon only Chase Phase Arena Phase
High Difficulty Save: Explosive Rounds
Weak Points
🎯
Chase Phase: No real weak point — do NOT dump ammo during the chase. He cannot be stopped, only slowed briefly.
🎯
Arena Phase: Mouth when he opens it during punish windows. Cyst on his back while downed.
💥
Environmental: Explosive canisters around the arena deal massive damage when he crowds you
Drops & Rewards
RPD Parking Garage Key
Requiem Rounds ×4 (high-tier ammo)
Story progression: unlocks East Raccoon City
Strategy

Chase Phase Do not waste ammo. Run until the upper-level arena opens. Use ladders to create distance — Mr. X is slow on stairs. Collect all ammo from side rooms while running, there is no penalty for exploring.

Arena Phase Use ladders to force Mr. X to change his movement trajectory, buying you angles to shoot the mouth during his attack animation. Explosive canisters in the arena corners deal roughly 3× normal gun damage — kite him near them when he's at half health.

When he goes to one knee (downed), sprint around and empty into the back cyst before he rises. This is your highest-damage window of the fight.

Boss #3 · Outside RPD — Final Tyrant Form
SUPER TYRANT
Mutated final form of Mr. X. Open-area fight outside the RPD building.
Leon 3 Phases Time-Sensitive
High Difficulty Use: Magnum + Rockets
Weak Points
🎯
Phases 1–2: The exposed heart on his chest. Always centered — aim straight on.
🎯
Phase 3: After the heart is shielded — shoot the arm blade until it breaks, then heart is exposed again
🚁
Event trigger: At 25% health, a helicopter airdrop provides Rocket Launcher ×1 — this is your kill shot
Drops & Rewards
Raccoon City Survivor Badge (key story item)
ARK Access Code — required to advance
Achievement: "Terminator" unlocked
Strategy

Phase 1–2 Save the Magnum ammo you collected from the RPD Evidence Locker safe. Dump Magnum rounds directly into the chest heart — this is the most efficient DPS in the game. Keep moving laterally; the Tyrant's grab attack is a one-shot on Insanity difficulty.

Phase 3 He shields the heart with his arm blade. Shoot the blade with any weapon until it shatters (takes ~12 handgun rounds). Heart exposed again — finish with Magnum.

Rocket Finish At 25% HP the helicopter event triggers. Pick up the Rocket Launcher immediately — it despawns after 30 seconds. One rocket to the chest ends the fight.

Insanity tip: His grab deals instant death. Prioritize dodging grabs over dealing damage. It's faster to spend 20 extra seconds kiting than to restart from a typewriter save.
Boss #4 · ARK — Scripted Encounter
MUTATED EMILY
Mandatory scripted encounter. Story-critical decision point — affects ending.
Leon Scripted Ending Branch
Low Difficulty ⚠ Ending Impact
Strategy & Story Note

This is a scripted encounter with no gameplay punishment. When prompted, shoot mutated Emily to progress — do not hesitate or skip the prompt.

The important detail: On the Release Elpis ending path, Leon makes clear he did not hit her vitals, and the ending shows Emily survived and was rescued. Shooting Emily here does not lock you into the bad ending — it is required for the good one.

Common mistake: Players hesitate or reload a save thinking this causes the bad ending. It does not. You must shoot Emily to proceed. The ending is determined by the Final Puzzle in the ARK Core, not this encounter.
Boss #5 · Rhodes Hill — Grace Campaign
THE KEEPER
Close-quarters duel. Former staff member turned B.O.W. Parry-centric fight.
Grace only Parry Mechanic 2 Phases
Hard Required: Hatchet
Weak Points
🎯
Phase 1: After a successful parry — 1-second answer window with the hatchet. Sharpen hatchet during every lull.
🎯
Phase 2: Left side when he favors right-hand attacks. His left is always exposed during 3-hit combo finishers.
Drops & Rewards
Keeper's Key — unlocks Sub-Basement area
Blood Vial ×6 (high-value crafting resource)
File: The Keeper's Journal
Strategy

Phase 1 This is a close-quarters duel, not a traditional arena fight. Parry is your primary tool — the window is tight (0.3 seconds before impact). After a successful parry, immediately answer with the hatchet. Sharpen the hatchet every time The Keeper pauses to reposition.

Phase 2 He begins mixing unparryable grab attacks — these are telegraphed by a brief shoulder dip. Dodge right on the shoulder dip, then punish his left side during the 3-hit combo finisher. Gun ammo is inefficient here; hatchet damage is significantly higher per action.

Optional · Wrenwood Hotel Basement
THE AMALGAM
Fused mass of multiple infected bodies. Not required but drops valuable upgrade material.
Optional Leon / Grace High Reward
Medium-Hard
Weak Points & Drops
🎯
Exposed vertebrae on the back. Only visible when it rears up — shoot during the 2-second window.
Drops
Crimson Skull Gem (sell for 12,000 CP or trade for upgrade)
Infected Blood ×8 (Grace's crafting resource)
Why Fight It?

The Crimson Skull Gem sells for 12,000 CP at the merchant — equivalent to roughly 3 weapon upgrades. On Insanity, this fight is harder but the gem value remains the same. Recommended on first playthrough.
