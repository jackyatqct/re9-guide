01 Hotel Lobby & Arrival Leon — Combat intro ▼
  • 01
    After the opening cutscene, take control of Leon at the hotel entrance. Head through the main doors — the front desk area is your first exploration zone.
  • 02
    Check behind the front desk for Handgun Ammo ×8. The Mr. Raccoon #01 statue is on the shelf behind the check-in monitor — shoot it now.
  • 03
    Open Safe #1 behind the reception counter. Code: R36 · L08 · R52 (Normal). Contains Shotgun Shells ×8 + Handgun Ammo ×12.
  • 04
    Examine the guest register on the desk for File #1. This introduces the game's lore — reads fast, worth skimming for context.
  • 05
    First infected enemy appears in the lobby hallway. This is your tutorial fight — practice the parry timing here before moving on. Parry window: 0.3s before impact.
  • 06
    Proceed through the door east of the front desk toward the Dining Room. Pick up the Typewriter Ribbon from the table near the hall entrance — this is your save resource on Insanity/Classic.
Key Items in this section
File #1 — Guest Register Handgun Ammo ×8 Shotgun Shells ×8 (safe) Typewriter Ribbon
02 Dining Room, Parlor & Roulette Puzzle Leon — First puzzle ▼
  • 01
    Enter the Dining Room. Shoot Mr. Raccoon #02 on the top shelf of the wine rack, far left corner — look up at the shelving unit.
  • 02
    Clear 3 infected in the dining room. Use hatchet finishers on staggered enemies to conserve ammo — after a headshot stagger, press melee for the hatchet kill.
  • 03
    Find Antique Coin #01 inside the small potted plant on the left side of reception (examine, don't just pass by).
  • 04
    Enter the Parlor through the west hallway door. The Roulette Wheel puzzle is here — examine the painting above the fireplace first to read the color sequence.
  • 05
    Roulette solution: RED — BLACK — RED — RED — BLACK. Interact with the wheel and spin to each color. Reward: Parlor Key + Antique Coin #04.
  • 06
    While in the Parlor, collect Antique Coin #05 from the bar counter and Antique Coin #06 by destroying the porcelain container behind the bar.
  • 07
    Use the Parlor Key to unlock the back room. Inside: Shotgun (W-870) — your most important early weapon. Pick it up.
Tip: Spend Antique Coins at the Parlor lockers now. Prioritize the Hip Pouch (3 coins) for extra inventory slots immediately — it makes the entire hotel section more comfortable.

03 Upper Floors (2F – 4F) Leon — Exploration ▼
  • 01
    Take the main staircase up to 2F. The hallway has 2 infected — use shotgun on the closer one, hatchet finish the second to save shells.
  • 02
    Room 204 (unlocked): contains a First Aid Spray and a note pointing you toward the roof. Room 207 is locked — you need the Master Key from the basement later.
  • 03
    3F: The Manager's Office has a typewriter save point — save here before proceeding if on Insanity/Classic mode.
  • 04
    4F fire escape access: head to the east end of the hallway, push through the emergency exit door. This leads to the roof.
  • 05
    Mr. Raccoon #05 is on the roof northeast corner near the water tower base. Shoot it before proceeding down the external fire stairs.
  • 06
    The roof also has a crate with Antique Coin #02 hidden behind the overturned soup tureen (technically connects to the dining room back exit). Collect it if missed earlier.
  • 07
    Pick up File #3 — Manager's Memo from the desk in the Manager's Office — it hints at the basement keycard location.
Don't miss: Room 207 requires the Master Key from the basement. Come back after getting it — it contains the Shotgun Stock upgrade which significantly improves handling.
04 Hotel Basement & Generator Room Leon — Pre-boss prep ▼
  • 01
    Take the stairwell down to B1. The basement corridor is dark — infected here react to sound, so walk (hold crouch-walk) past the first two unless you need blood samples (Grace players).
  • 02
    Mr. Raccoon #03 is in the Generator Room on the pipe junction near the east wall — it's behind the large pipe, easy to miss. Shoot it before the boss fight area.
  • 03
    Find the Basement Master Key hanging on the hook inside the generator room. This unlocks Room 207 upstairs — go back for the Shotgun Stock if you want it now.
  • 04
    Storage room adjacent to generator: Antique Coin #03 inside a cardboard box near the south shelving. Destroy the box.
  • 05
    Optional boss: The Amalgam is accessible through the locked south door (Basement Key from Room 207). High reward — Crimson Skull Gem worth 12,000 CP. Fight it if you have shells to spare.
  • 06
    Proceed through the boiler room corridor to reach the pre-boss staging area. A typewriter is just before the Chunk arena — save here.
  • 07
    Stock up: you want at least 12 Shotgun Shells and 2 Flash Grenades going into the Chunk fight. Craft if needed using gunpowder from the shelves nearby.
Key Items
Basement Master Key Antique Coin #03 Mr. Raccoon #03 Typewriter (save point)
05 Boss Fight: Chunk Leon & Grace — Area boss ▼
  • 01
    Phase 1: Stay mobile and circle the arena. Chunk telegraphs his charge 1.5 seconds before executing — the pull-back animation is your cue. Fire 2 shotgun blasts into his exposed chest during the wind-up.
  • 02
    After ~4 shotgun blasts, he enters Phase 2 — a glowing mutation appears on his back. Do NOT waste ammo on his front now.
  • 03
    Phase 2: Throw a Flash Grenade to stagger him, then sprint around to his back and dump 3–4 shotgun shells into the glowing mass. Repeat once more to finish the fight.
  • 04
    Grace players only: Craft 3 Hemolytic Injectors before the fight (kill the 3 zombies in the hallway outside the boss room for infected blood). Injecting all 3 when the back mutation is exposed ends Phase 2 instantly — saves all your ammo.
  • 05
    After Chunk dies, loot Chunk's Keycard from his body — required to exit the basement. Leon also receives the Hatchet: Mortal Edge upgrade — unequip your current hatchet to equip it.
  • 06
    Use Chunk's Keycard on the locked door at the far end of the arena. Cutscene plays, transitioning to Rhodes Hill.
Full boss guide: See the Chunk Boss Strategy page for detailed phase breakdowns, weak points, and Insanity difficulty tips.

01 Care Center Reception & Blood Crafting Intro Grace — Mechanic introduction ▼
  • 01
    Grace arrives at the care center entrance. Unlike Leon, her default pistol has limited ammo — prioritize stealth over direct combat wherever possible.
  • 02
    Mr. Raccoon #06 is immediately visible on the left window of the reception area, facing outside. Shoot it from the entrance hallway.
  • 03
    The Blood Crafting tutorial triggers here — kill the infected behind the reception desk to collect Infected Blood ×3. Open the crafting menu and create a Blood Coagulant (healing item).
  • 04
    Collect Antique Coin #07 from the waiting room — under the right-side chair. Crouch and examine the floor.
  • 05
    First typewriter save point is in the side room off the reception lobby. Save here — the care center is large and you don't want to redo the arrival section.
  • 06
    Head through the double doors marked "Ward A" to proceed to the East Wing stairwell.
Grace players: Don't skip blood collection. Infected Blood is your primary crafting resource for healing items, door-opening chemicals, and combat injectors. Kill enemies in every room before moving on — you'll need the stockpile.
02 East Wing (2F) & Nurse's Station Grace — Exploration & safe ▼
  • 01
    Take the Ward A stairwell to 2F. The East Wing hallway has 4 infected — use stealth to sneak past the first two, then deal with the pair near the nurses' station (they can't see you from behind the nursing cart).
  • 02
    Safe #2 is behind the nurses' desk. Code: L22 · R50 · L14 (Normal) / R68 · L34 · R90 (Insanity). Contains Ink Ribbon ×2 + First Aid Spray.
  • 03
    Collect Antique Coin #08 from the nurses' station counter, right side.
  • 04
    Mr. Raccoon #07 is inside a medical supply cart in the hallway — the cart door requires the East Wing Keycard, which you get later from the Quartz Door puzzle. Come back for this one.
  • 05
    Find File #12 — Patient Transfer Record in the examination room — it hints at the Director's Office location and gives background on the outbreak.
  • 06
    Exit the East Wing through the connecting corridor north to reach the Central Hall, where the Quartz Door is located.
03 Director's Office & Quartz Door Puzzle Grace — Key puzzle sequence ▼
  • 01
    The Director's Office is on 3F West Wing. Take the Central Hall staircase up — there's a Banshee-type enemy near the 3F landing. Back away to trigger its patrol pattern, then slip past when it turns.
  • 02
    Safe #3 in the Director's Office. Code: R04 · L72 · R38 (Normal) / L16 · R60 · L82 (Insanity). Contains Hip Pouch upgrade — expands inventory by 2 slots.
  • 03
    Mr. Raccoon #08 is on the Director's bookshelf, 3rd shelf from the top, right side. It's small — scan the shelves carefully.
  • 04
    Examine the puzzle safe in the Chairman's Office (adjacent to Director's Office) to collect Moon Quartz and Antique Coin #09 from the bookcase near the window.
  • 05
    Return to the Quartz Door in Central Hall. Insert the Moon Quartz and input the sequence: Moon → Sun → Star → Moon. Reward: East Wing Keycard + Upgrade item.
  • 06
    Now backtrack to 2F East Wing to collect Mr. Raccoon #07 from the supply cart using the East Wing Keycard.
Quartz Door shortcut: You can go directly to the Director's Office without clearing the entire 2F first. The Quartz Door unlocks a significant shortcut back through the care center — solve it before exploring the Basement.
04 Sterilization Chamber & Basement Grace — Resource-critical section ▼
  • 01
    Take the service elevator from 1F to B1. The Sterilization Chamber is straight ahead — there's a large enemy patrolling. Sneak along the left wall to avoid it.
  • 02
    Safe #4 is on the window side of the Sterilization Chamber. Sneak past the enemy to reach it. Code: R10 · L90 · R20 (Normal) / L80 · R50 · L00 (Insanity). Contains Stabilizer.
  • 03
    Mr. Raccoon #09 is on a trolley in the far corner of the Sterilization Chamber, behind the enemy. Sneak or kill the enemy first, then collect.
  • 04
    The Chemical Storage room is accessible from the south corridor — pick up the Bottle of Acid from the table. This is used to dissolve the door lock to the Sub-Basement later.
  • 05
    Collect File #71 — Sterilization Chamber Safe note (the in-game hint for Safe #4) and Antique Coin #10 from the Morgue examination table (B2, accessible from the service stairs).
  • 06
    Proceed through the locked Sub-Basement door using the Acid Bottle. This leads to The Keeper boss fight area.
05 Boss Fight: The Keeper Grace only — Parry duel ▼
  • 01
    The Keeper is a close-quarters duel, not a traditional arena fight. Your hatchet and parry system are your primary tools — guns are inefficient here.
  • 02
    Phase 1: Watch for his attack animations. Parry the moment before impact (0.3 second window). A successful parry creates a 1-second hatchet window — immediately answer with melee. Sharpen the hatchet during every pause.
  • 03
    Phase 2: He begins mixing unparryable grab attacks, telegraphed by a brief left shoulder dip. On shoulder dip — dodge right, then punish his exposed left side during the 3-hit combo finisher.
  • 04
    After defeating him, collect Keeper's Key from his body. This unlocks the Sub-Basement storage room with Mr. Raccoon #10 and Antique Coin #11.
Full boss guide: See The Keeper Boss Strategy for complete phase analysis and Insanity tips.
Boss Drops
Keeper's Key Blood Vial ×6 File: The Keeper's Journal
06 Sub-Basement & Exit Grace — Area wrap-up ▼
  • 01
    Use the Keeper's Key to access the Sub-Basement flooded storage room. Mr. Raccoon #10 is on the walkway north wall, just above the waterline.
  • 02
    Mr. Raccoon #11 is in the Morgue B2 — inside an open drawer, third row from the bottom, left side. Open all drawers in that row.
  • 03
    Collect Antique Coin #11 from the shelf in the flooded storage room.
  • 04
    The ARK Access Elevator is at the end of the Sub-Basement. Interact with the terminal — cutscene plays, transitioning to Raccoon City.
Before leaving Rhodes Hill: Make sure you have Mr. Raccoons #06–11, Antique Coins #07–11, and all Files from this area. The care center is not revisitable after the transition to Raccoon City.

01 Raccoon City Streets Leon — Open exploration ▼
  • 01
    Leon arrives in the nuked ruins of Raccoon City. The street area is semi-open — explore before heading to the RPD. There are side streets to the north and south with supplies.
  • 02
    Mr. Raccoon #12 is on a fire escape platform 2F level in the NW building cluster. Shoot it from across the street.
  • 03
    Find Antique Coin #12 inside a newspaper box near the first barricade — shoot the box to open it.
  • 04
    The Merchant's Stall is in the alley east of the main street. Use accumulated CP to upgrade your shotgun — the Reload Speed upgrade is the best value here for the upcoming RPD section.
  • 05
    Head toward the RPD building at the north end of the main street. The large front doors are your entry point.
02 Raccoon Police Department Leon — Mr. X begins pursuit ▼
  • 01
    Enter the RPD main lobby. Mr. Raccoon #13 is on top of the RPD crest mosaic on the center floor — look down from the entrance. Shoot it before proceeding.
  • 02
    Collect Antique Coin #13 from the first aid kit mounted on the lobby wall.
  • 03
    Head down to the Generator Room (B1) and solve the Power Grid Puzzle: flip switches in order C → A → D → B. Wrong order spawns enemies and triggers the alarm.
  • 04
    With power restored, access the Evidence Room (B1). Open Safe #5: R28 · L66 · R42 (Normal). Contains Magnum Rounds ×5 — save these for Super Tyrant, do not use them yet.
  • 05
    Mr. Raccoon #14 is inside Evidence Box #7 on the shelf — interact with the box, not just the shelf surface.
  • 06
    Collect Antique Coin #14 from the RPD Armory gear locker — requires the RPD Armory Key found on an Officer corpse in the 1F east hallway.
  • 07
    Triggering the Security Room on 2F initiates the Mr. X chase sequence. Make sure you've collected everything before entering that room.
SAVE the Magnum Rounds from Safe #5. They're for the Super Tyrant boss fight — the Magnum is the most efficient weapon against his exposed heart. Using them on regular enemies will make the boss significantly harder.
03 Boss: Mr. X Chase & Arena Fight Leon only — Two phases ▼
  • 01
    Chase Phase: Do NOT waste ammo. Mr. X cannot be stopped — only slowed temporarily. Run toward the upper-level arena, collecting ammo from side rooms as you go. Use ladders to create distance on stairs.
  • 02
    Mr. Raccoon #16 is on the NF Building Rooftop during the chase — NW corner near the A/C unit. This is timed — shoot it while running or you'll miss it for this playthrough.
  • 03
    Arena Phase: Once the upper arena opens, the real fight begins. Shoot his mouth during punish windows (after big swing attacks). Use ladders to force movement angles.
  • 04
    When Mr. X goes to one knee (downed), sprint to his back and empty ammo into the back cyst — highest damage window of the fight. He rises after ~3 seconds.
  • 05
    Kite him near the explosive canisters in the arena corners at half health — they deal 3× normal gun damage when he's adjacent. One canister + shotgun blast can take ~20% of his health.
04 Underground Sewers Leon — Pre-final boss prep ▼
  • 01
    After defeating Mr. X, take the Parking Garage Key from his body. Exit through the RPD parking garage to reach the sewer access.
  • 02
    Mr. Raccoon #15 is in the sewers on a ledge above the first water valve puzzle — jump up using the crates to the east.
  • 03
    Open Safe #6 in the Sewers Control Room: L12 · R84 · L56 (Normal). Contains the Scope attachment for Leon's Sniper Rifle — useful for the rooftop section ahead.
  • 04
    Collect Antique Coin #15 from the desk next to the control panel in the Control Room.
  • 05
    The sewer exit brings you to the Central Square — the Super Tyrant arena. Save at the typewriter before exiting. Stock to 12+ Shotgun Shells and make sure Magnum Rounds are in your inventory.
05 Boss Fight: Super Tyrant & Area Exit Leon — Area finale ▼
  • 01
    Before the fight triggers: Mr. Raccoon #17 is at the base of the war memorial statue in Central Square. Grab it now — it disappears after the boss fight starts.
  • 02
    Phases 1–2: Dump all 5 Magnum Rounds into his exposed chest heart. Move laterally at all times — his grab attack is instant-kill on Insanity.
  • 03
    Phase 3: He shields his heart with his arm blade. Shoot the blade with any weapon (~12 handgun rounds) until it shatters — then switch back to Magnum for the exposed heart.
  • 04
    At 25% HP: A helicopter airdrop lands a Rocket Launcher ×1. Pick it up immediately — it despawns after 30 seconds. One rocket to the chest ends the fight.
  • 05
    After the fight: collect the Raccoon City Survivor Badge and ARK Access Code from his remains. Cutscene plays — transition to The ARK.
Full boss guide: See Super Tyrant Boss Strategy for phase-by-phase breakdown and Insanity difficulty notes.

The ARK is the most linear area of the game. Both protagonists are playable here. Contains a Point of No Return before the final boss — make sure you've collected everything before passing through. The ending is determined by the Final Puzzle, not earlier choices.
⚠ POINT OF NO RETURN: The ARK Core Checkpoint door is irreversible. Before entering the final sector, ensure you have collected all Mr. Raccoons (#18–22), all Antique Coins, and all Files from the ARK. You cannot go back after this point.

01 ARK Hangar & Entry Both characters — Area arrival ▼
  • 01
    Both Leon and Grace arrive at the ARK separately. The Hangar is a large open area — clear it thoroughly before moving on. Enemies here are faster and more aggressive than earlier areas.
  • 02
    Mr. Raccoon #18 is on top of a cargo container near the east entrance. Climb the smaller containers to reach the larger one — it's on the flat top surface.
  • 03
    Collect Antique Coin #16 near the hangar entrance on a red equipment cart.
  • 04
    The Merchant has a stall in the southwest corner of the hangar — this is your last upgrade opportunity before the endgame. Prioritize Magnum damage upgrades if available.
  • 05
    Proceed through the blast doors to the Research Lab section.
02 Research Labs A & B Both characters — Key safe + collectibles ▼
  • 01
    Mr. Raccoon #19 is in Lab A inside the specimen containment unit. The Lab Keycard to open it is found in the same room — check the desk near the entrance.
  • 02
    Mr. Raccoon #20 is in Lab B on the typewriter desk itself — it's right at the save point, hard to miss. Save here before collecting it.
  • 03
    Collect Antique Coin #17 from the sample freezer in Lab A, second compartment.
  • 04
    Open Safe #7 in Lab B (near the typewriter): R92 · L24 · R46 (Normal) / L38 · R70 · L16 (Insanity). Contains Override Manual + Steroids — the most valuable single safe in the game.
  • 05
    Collect Files #68 and #69 (Product Catalogs 1 & 2) from Lab B — these are the clues for the Final Puzzle. Read them carefully.
03 Control Tower & Point of No Return Both characters — Last collectible chance ▼
  • 01
    Mr. Raccoon #21 is on Control Tower Level 3, behind the main console panel. Crouch to see it behind the desk.
  • 02
    The ARK Core Corridor leads to the Point of No Return door. Before approaching it: Mr. Raccoon #22 is on a pipe junction 20m before the door — this is your last chance if on Insanity.
  • 03
    FINAL CHECKLIST before the Point of No Return door: Mr. Raccoons #18–22 collected ✓ | All Antique Coins ✓ | Files #68 & #69 in inventory ✓ | Ammo stocked ✓
  • 04
    Enter the ARK Core Checkpoint door when ready. This transition is permanent.
04 Boss: Mutated Emily & Core Chamber Leon — Scripted encounter ▼
  • 01
    Just past the Point of No Return door: collect Mr. Raccoon #23 on the left wall immediately on entry, and Mr. Raccoon #24 on the second console tier facing Grace's terminal.
  • 02
    Mr. Raccoon #25 is hidden inside the broken monitor on the west wall — shoot the screen to reveal it, then shoot the statue.
  • 03
    The Mutated Emily encounter is fully scripted. When prompted, shoot her — do not hesitate or reload a save. This does NOT cause the bad ending. It is required for the good ending.
  • 04
    After the encounter, both Leon and Grace reconvene at the Final Puzzle terminals.
Common mistake: Players reload thinking shooting Emily causes the bad ending. It does not. The ending is 100% determined by the Final Puzzle inputs below. Shoot her to proceed.
05 Final Puzzle & Game Ending Both characters — Ending branch ▼
  • 01
    Two terminals appear: one for Grace, one for Leon. Both must be solved to get the true ending (Release Elpis). In solo mode, Leon's terminal auto-locks after Grace completes hers — solve Grace first.
  • 02
    Grace's Terminal (True Ending): BLOOD → MEMORY → REQUIEM → REBIRTH
  • 03
    Leon's Terminal (True Ending): TRUTH → SACRIFICE → ORDER → RELEASE
  • 04
    Both correct inputs trigger the Release Elpis ending cutscene — the true ending. Duration: approximately 12 minutes. Shows Emily's survival and the aftermath.
  • 05
    After the credits: New Game+ unlocks with all weapons and upgrades carried over. Insanity difficulty also unlocks if not already available. Post-game CP farming and infinite ammo unlocks are now accessible.
