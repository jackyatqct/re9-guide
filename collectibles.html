Antique Coins

Collect Antique Coins and exchange them at the locked lockers in the Parlor for four powerful upgrades. You need 17 total coins to unlock all four lockers.

Priority order: Hip Pouch first (more inventory = more resources carried), then Stabilizer (reduces recoil), Steroids (increases Grace's health permanently), Override Manual (weapon range upgrade).

Upgrades

Coin Exchange — Parlor Lockers

The 4 locked lockers in the Parlor cost a total of 17 Antique Coins. Unlock them in priority order below.

Upgrade Cost Effect Priority
Hip Pouch 3 Coins Adds 2 inventory slots. Stack up to 3 times total across the game. Get First
Stabilizer 4 Coins Permanently reduces weapon recoil. Improves accuracy at range. Get Second
Steroids 4 Coins Permanently increases Grace's max health. Significant survivability boost. Get Third
Override Manual 6 Coins Unlocks weapon modification override — allows equipping incompatible parts. Get Fourth
